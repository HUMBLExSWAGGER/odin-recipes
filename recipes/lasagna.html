Lasagna

Prep:30min Cook:2.5hrs Total:3hrs

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat: Beef, Sausage, or Both
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Tomato: Crushed, Sauce, and Paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices: Parsley, Basil, Salt, Italian, Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese: Parmeasan, Mozzarella, Ricotta
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil
  6. Bake at 350F
