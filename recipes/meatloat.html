Meatloaf

Prep:30min Cook:1hr Total:1.5hr

This traditional meatloaf recipe made with saut√©ed vegetables, spices, and bread crumbs is moist and f lavorful and topped with an old-fashioned glaze.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Vegetables
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Egg
  • Spices
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Brown Sugar
  • Ketchup
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Sriracha

Directions

  1. Chop the vegetables
  2. Combine the chopped vegetables, ground beef, worcestershire sauce, spices, bread crumbs, and egg
  3. Bake at 350F for 15min
  4. Combine brown sugar, ketchup, and mustard; use as glaze, continue to bake until internal temp is 160F, about 1hr
